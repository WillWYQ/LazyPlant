Introduction

This project is designed for individuals who want to maintain a green environment at home but have a busy lifestyle. It is targeted towards those who love plants but have limited time for manual care. Our primary objective is to create a safe setting for plant development by controlling soil moisture, providing supplementary light when necessary, and monitoring temperature conditions. The project aims to offer a novel solution for those who prioritize eco-friendliness in their homes but struggle to find time for plant maintenance.

Objectives and Specifications

Objectives

  • Through the LCD screen, we can observe detailed information about the plant, including the values of several significant indexes (temperature, humidity, illumination intensity)
  • Applying sensors to obtain the corresponding values (values we mentioned above), then we deliver the signal to those devices.
    • Watering

      When receiving the signal from the humidity sensor, the motor in the water tank will start and extract water from the tank to increase the humidity of the soil and the air.

    • Temperature

      If the microcontroller receives the signal from the temperature sensor, the current passes through the resistive heater, then creating the amount of heat in the outer of the flower pot.

    • Lighting

      The light sensor will work when the natural lighting is too dark if the sensor captures the environment is dark enough, the signal will transfer to the LED light, and the light will start to offer the light at the top of the flower pot.

  • Using Bluetooth to connect the entire system to the computer, so that users can use other devices to control the system to help them their ideal environment for the plants,depending on different plants.

Specification

Features

  1. Soil Moisture Management:
    1. Automatic Irrigation Adjustment:
      • Implementation of an automatic irrigation system that dynamically adjusts watering based on real-time soil moisture levels.
    1. Customizable Moisture Thresholds:
      • Customization of moisture thresholds to accommodate the specific moisture requirements of different plant types.
  1. Lighting Control:
    1. Ambient Light Monitoring:
      • Monitor ambient light conditions.
    1. Supplementary LED Lighting:
      • Supplementary LED lighting to maintain optimal light intensity or duration.
        • Adjustable light intensity, enabling customization to suit the specific needs of different plant species.
        • Automatic extension of lighting duration if natural light fails to meet the plants' requirements.
    1. Automated Light Intensity Control:
      • Automated light intensity adjustments tailored to predefined profiles for different plant varieties.
    1. Manual Light Intensity Control:
      • User-defined control allows for manual adjustments of light intensity based on individual preferences or specific plant observations.
  1. Temperature Regulation:
    1. Environmental and Soil Temperature Monitoring:
      • Monitor both environmental and soil temperatures in real time.
    1. Indirect Heating or Cooling of Soil Temperature:
      • Indirectly adjusting soil temperature, ensuring optimal conditions for plant growth.
  1. User Interface:
    1. LCD Screen for Real-time Environmental Data Display:
      • Integration of an LCD screen to provide users with real-time insights into the plant environment.
      • Display of current temperature and moisture levels.
      • Summary of temperature and moisture trends.
      • Presentation of today's cumulative light exposure duration.
      • Indication of the number of days elapsed since sowing for enhanced monitoring.
    1. Control Button for Preference Settings:
      • Control through Computer Programs or Control Buttons
      • Enabling users to set and customize their preferred environmental settings with ease.
